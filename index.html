Sofia

Sofia is the capital city and the largest city in Bulgaria. It is situated at a strategic crossroads, connecting the Western Europe to the Middle and Middle East countries between the Danube coast and the Aegean Sea, between the Black Sea and the Adriatic.The Vitosha Mountain, a wonderful place for rest and tourism, is in the immediate vicinity.

Plovdiv

Plovdiv is the second largest city with more than 8000 years of history. It was chosen as the European Capital of Culture for 2019. The Architectural Reserve of Ancient Plovdiv is an excellently preserved complex in which we can walk on a small area over the different ages.

Burgas

The city has won the Best Dwelling City Award three times. In its surroundings, there are several large lakes that serve as the habitat of rare birds.Above them passes Via Pontica - one of the main paths for bird migration between Europe and Africa.

Varna

Varna is the sea capital of Bulgaria. Here is found the oldest processed gold in the world 4400bc. Today, Varna is a modern city with a rich cultural calendar.

Veliko Tarnovo
